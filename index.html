Frequently Asked Questions
What is link building and why does it matter for SEO? ▼
Link building is the process of acquiring backlinks from other websites to your own. These links act as “votes of confidence,” signaling to search engines that your content is valuable and authoritative. High-quality backlinks help improve your domain authority and increase your chances of ranking higher in search results.
What are the main types of backlinks that SEOLinkHub creates? ▼
Link Insertions (Niche Edits) – Adding backlinks to existing high-quality content on trusted sites.
Guest Post Links – Publishing articles with backlinks on relevant, authoritative blogs.
Editorial Links – Naturally placed links within content (often acquired via PR and outreach).
Why do I need to hire a link building agency? ▼
A link building agency has a huge database of domains, many SEO connections, and is more cost-effective. Instead of hiring an in-house team and spending enormous time and effort on the campaign, it’s best to simply outsource.
How long does it take to see results from a link building agency? ▼
Usually, link building results start coming in during the first month. However, this depends on a lot of factors, including your website’s current state, the number and quality of backlinks you’re getting, the consistency you keep, and more.
How long does it take to see results?▼
It will take at least 6 months to start seeing significant results from any link building campaign. However, this depends on a lot of factors, including your website’s current state, the number and quality of backlinks you’re getting, the consistency you keep, and more. Remember, link building is a long-term process.
How do backlinks improve my website’s Google rankings? ▼
Google views backlinks as endorsements. When a reputable site links to yours, it passes authority (SEOLinkHub), boosting your website’s credibility and helping it rank higher. The more relevant and high-quality backlinks you have, the stronger your SEO performance.
How does SEOLinkHub charging works? ▼
You only pay for each successfully placed backlink—no retainers, hidden fees, or unnecessary commitments.
Can you guarantee the #1 spot on Google? ▼
Absolutely not – and no self-respecting link-building agency ever should. While we can help boost your site’s domain authority and help pages rank better in the search results, no one can guarantee a specific ranking.
How long will my backlinks remain active? ▼
Backlinks are guaranteed to remain active for at least one year. While most links will stay live for many years, some loss may occur over time as blog owners may stop maintaining their sites or change their focus.
